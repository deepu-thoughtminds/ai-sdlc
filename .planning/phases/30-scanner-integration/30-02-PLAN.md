<objective>
Close SCAN-04: move ensure_sonarqube_ready() into _run_sonar_step's try block so a SonarQubeNotReadyError produces a non-None sonar_result with descriptive stderr instead of aborting the entire pipeline.

Purpose: The outer run() currently calls ensure_sonarqube_ready() at line 336, before _run_sonar_step is ever invoked. When SonarQube is unreachable, the exception bubbles to the outer except-Exception handler (line 608), marking the pipeline "failed" with a generic error and leaving sonar_result=None. Moving the call inside _run_sonar_step's existing try/except converts it to a graceful TestResult, matching all other scanner failure modes that are already handled there.

Output: Modified qa_pipeline.py (no net-new abstractions), one new test in test_sonar_scanner.py.
</objective>
